Tuna & Chickpea Salad

Chickpea Tuna Salad with salty capers

Ingredients

  • 15 ounce canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 6 ounce can wild tuna, drained
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons chopped red onion
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • Chopped lettuce

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients
  2. Adjust vinegar
  3. Add brine
  4. Season to taste
