Baked Wonton Chips

Ingredients

  • 12 refrigerated wonton dough
  • olive oil spray
  • black and white sesame seeds
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F
  2. Spray a sheet pan with oil
  3. Cut wontons in half on the diagonal
  4. Place on the sheet pan and spritz with more oil
  5. Season with sesame seeds and salt
  6. Bake for 8 minutes, untill golden and crisp
